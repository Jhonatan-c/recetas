Arroz Con Leche

Description

It's a dish that is used to give to guests at a party and to make in a short time if perhaps you don't want to cook.

Ingredients

  1. Rice
  2. Milk
  3. Cinnamon
  4. Raisins
  5. Cheese

Steps

  • 2 glasses of water
  • 1 glass of rice
  • 2 glasses of milk
  • Stir until homogeneous
