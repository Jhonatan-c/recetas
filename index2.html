Ajiaco

  • Inicio

Description

It's a typical Colombian dish and it is too tasty, I know that if you make it you will like it

Ingredients

  1. Cob
  2. Chicken
  3. Potatoes
  4. Water
  5. Milk
  6. Milk crem

Steps

  • Heat the water until it boils
  • Leave the chicken in water for 30 minutes
  • Shred the chicken
  • Heat the cob
  • Cut the potatoes and let them cook in water
